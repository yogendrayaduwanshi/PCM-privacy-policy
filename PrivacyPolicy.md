# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 31st Dec 2025

PCM respects your privacy and is designed to operate without collecting or storing personal user data.

---

## Information Collection and Use

PCM does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers.  
Any permissions requested by the app (such as phone or contacts access) are used only to provide core functionality and are processed locally on the user’s device.

---

## Data Sharing

PCM does not share user data with third parties.

---

## Data Security

All data processing occurs locally on the device.  
No user information is uploaded, stored remotely, or shared outside the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

PCM does not use third-party services that collect or track user data.

---

## Children’s Privacy

PCM is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
